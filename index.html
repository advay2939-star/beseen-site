Your privacy is paramount to us. All photos you upload are encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. This is then delivered to you via WhatsApp within 24 hours — often much sooner. Your expert will also introduce themselves and let you know they're available to support you.
Most customers notice an improvement in skin texture and feel within the first 7–10 days. Concerns like acne, oiliness, and dryness typically show visible improvement by the 21-day mark — which is why we have a structured 21-day progress review built in. Pigmentation and fine lines take longer — usually 4–8 weeks of consistent use. Your expert will help calibrate your expectations and adjust your routine at the 21-day checkpoint if needed. Consistency is the most important factor, and we're here to make that easy.
